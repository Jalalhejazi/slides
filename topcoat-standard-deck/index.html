Topcoat

CSS for building clean and fast web apps.

Problems we hear, and have.

  • → Performance.
  • → Maintenance, and extending.
  • → Upgrading and new platforms.

Further motivations.

  • → Building complex applications is difficult, time consuming, and hard to maintain.
  • → Internal teams at Adobe should work from a common, and consistent, foundation.
  • → PhoneGap developers get a quickstart designed with their problem space in mind.

Performance first!

Design goals and principles.

    Discreet.

    Compose builds down to the components and platforms supported.

      Modular.

      Layout is seperate from reset is seperate from aesthetic.

        Extensible.

        Intellegent tooling, enabling unlimited theming. Craftsmanship: encouraged.

          Friendly.

          No DOM library, or any client, dependency. Pure, simple, and readable, CSS.

            Open and free.

            Apache 2 License, everything on Github.
